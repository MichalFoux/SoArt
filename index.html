SoArt Movement

SoArt is a global networked and digital movement that connects people, knowledge, initiatives, and resources so art can become a living force for healing, community, learning, and social transformation.

Join the movement Who we are

In a century shaped by displacement, trauma, polarization, inequality, and the erosion of belonging, art offers more than expression. It creates meaning, restores trust, opens dialogue, and helps people move from experience to shared action.

Why SoArt

From fragmented initiatives to a connected global field

Across the world, artists, educators, therapists, social entrepreneurs, activists, researchers, and communities are using creative practice to strengthen dignity, resilience, identity, participation, and hope.

Yet much of this work remains local, fragmented, undocumented, and invisible. SoArt is being formed as a shared global infrastructure for connection, documentation, learning, recognition, collaboration, and cumulative impact.

Four dimensions of action

How art creates change

Community

Community Art

Art created within community contexts, by and with community members, to strengthen belonging, voice, participation, and shared identity.

Capacity

Human Capacity Building

Creative processes that strengthen agency, resilience, self-expression, confidence, identity, and personal growth.

Action

Action Art

Art used intentionally as civic and activist action to influence public agendas, mobilize participation, and challenge social realities.

Dialogue

Awareness Art

Art that opens dialogue, shifts narratives, creates empathy, and builds spaces of understanding between groups.
